FAQ

Little answers, no essay.

Can I change the colors?

Yup — open styles.css and edit the :root variables.

How do I add more drinks?

Copy one .menu-item block in the menu section and paste it.

How do the pearls float?

The JS creates tiny circles and animates them upward with CSS. You can pause it with the button.
